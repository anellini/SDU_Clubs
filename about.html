about us

We will make your student period very eventful and memorable!

Hi, SDUdent. We invite you, yes, you to join any club that you want. Do you like to draw? Become a member of the art club. Do you like to sing and play on instruments? Become a member of the music club. Every month we will provide a calendar of events. I hope you will rest and come back full of strength and energy to conquer new peaks.

At the bottom we leave a field for filling out the questionnaire and leave a request for any club you like.
